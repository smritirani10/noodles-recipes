  1. take vegtable like:onion,carrot,beetroot,capsicum,green chilli,tomato,beans
  2. take noodles masala
  3. take soya sauce,red chilli sauce,tomato sauce
  4. take eggs
  5. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame. Add the ginger garlic paste and fry for 20 to 30 seconds. Saute the onions, beans and green chillies for a minute or two.     6. Serve hot.
